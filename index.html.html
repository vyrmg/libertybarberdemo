Our Story

Old Fashion.
Real Results.

Liberty Barber Shop has been a fixture in downtown Libertyville since 1916. Tucked right in the heart of Milwaukee Avenue, we're a straight-talking, no-frills barbershop that's been keeping the men of Lake County sharp for over a century.

Family-run since 1950, nothing fancy about us — no appointments, no women's hair, no blow-dry stations. Just skilled barbers, a comfortable chair, and a cut that speaks for itself. Cash only, the way it's always been.
